FAQs

Participating in this project can be hard!
This page is designed to answer frequently asked questions.
Feel free to ask any additional questions through email.

What form do the “memories to savor” have to be in?

The form can be in any shape or format, which includes a booklet, scrapbook, or collage. Creativity that breaks conventional examples is welcomed!

What should be included in “memories to savor”?

“Memories to savor” booklet should include pictures, information, and sayings about the recipient. Personal memories and experiences are also welcomed, so feel free to adorn “memories to savor” with something you like! However, endeavor to avoid subjects related to religion.

Can I write “memories to savor” in an online form?

Totally! Creating an online form makes the process a lot quicker and easier! Just send the PDF form of the work via email enlisted on the website.
