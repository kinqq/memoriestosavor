Our Mission

Founded in 2022, Memories to Savor endeavors to support patients who suffer from memory loss. Our mission is to remind patients of their values and characters through adorning and compiling their memories that want to be savored, providing a little gift that can bring a little impact to their lives.

Participate

By receiving pieces of personal memories from the patients, you can recreate the memories in the form of a booklet or a scrapbook, decorating them with pictures, drawings, and sayings. Please click to "join" button below to participate in this program. For more information, click the "Participate" navigation button at the top.
